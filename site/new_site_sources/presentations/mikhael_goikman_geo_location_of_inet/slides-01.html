What Businesses want

  • To learn everything about their existing clients, all their habits.
  • To find potential clients and place advertisements effectively.
  • To create a marketing strategy that leads to more purchases.

Fortunately most of the businesses are not evil, just a bit annoying.

Unsuccessful strategy:

  • Suggesting everyone is a potential client (never true).
  • SPAM (may be successful sometimes with commodity, discounts).
  • Creating a decorative site without a content.

Successful strategy:

  • Learning what the client search for and purchase.
  • Targeted advertisements (Google AdWords).
  • Making a site more accessible to the client's needs.
