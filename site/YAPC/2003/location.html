Car

There is no car access allowed into the university and parking outside the university is very complicated. Therefore, we encourage participants to arrive by public transportation such as train, bus and taxi.

Car Pool

Some people might want to share a car. Send e-mail about such request or offer to the mailing list.

Bus

Egged bus company in Hebrew.

Train

Israel Railways: Hebrew and in English

From Tel Aviv
Take one of the trains that leave at the following times from Tel Aviv Central station. (Also know as the north bus station or Arlozorov) 07:21, 07:40, 08:04.
Get off at Hof-Hacarmel station. (Matam)
Travel time is about 40-60 minutes.
Take a taxi from Hof-HaCarmel.

Taxi

A taxi from Hof Ha-Carmel train station costs about 25-35 NIS and it takes about 30-40 minutes.
At the train station try looking for other people attending the conference and share a taxi.
Usually there are taxis next to the station but if not call one of the following TAXI companies:
Keniyon Haifa 04-8500886
Kastra 04-8590055
